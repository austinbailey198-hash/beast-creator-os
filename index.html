Feastables · Creator Brief

Deluxe Dark Almond · Summer Drop

Make the first bite look as good as it tastes. We want real reactions from people your audience already trusts on food, not an ad read.

Creator · Keith Lee Format · 1× TikTok + 1× IG Reel Go-live · Aug 14, 7pm ET Fee · [locked in contract] Usage · 60-day whitelist

The one thing

If a viewer remembers one thing: Deluxe is the real-chocolate upgrade. 55% cacao, whole roasted almonds, nothing fake. Everything else supports that.

Talking points

  • Honest first-bite reaction, your real rating scale.
  • Snap the bar on camera. The clean break is the proof of quality.
  • "Made with better stuff." Real cacao, real almonds, no palm oil.
  • Where to grab it: Walmart + feastables.com, this week.

Product truth (don't overclaim)

  • 55% cacao dark chocolate, whole roasted almonds.
  • Fair Trade cocoa. Say "better ingredients," not "healthy."
  • Contains almonds + milk. Show nothing as allergen-free.

Deliverables

▶ 1× TikTok, 30-60s, native taste-test
▶ 1× IG Reel cross-post, same cut OK
◍ 2× Story frames w/ product link sticker
◷ Cut #1 due Aug 9 · 1 revision round

Guardrails

Do
  • #ad or "paid partnership" tag on. FTC requires it
  • Keep your voice; script is a starting point
  • Good natural light on the snap moment
  • Tag @feastables + @beast
Don't
  • No health/diet claims
  • No competitor call-outs by name
  • No kids as the focus of the video
  • Don't bury the disclosure in the caption

Why you

Your audience takes your food takes as gospel. The honest-rating format is the ad. We're not asking you to sell; we're asking you to react.

Run of show
Wk 1-2

Lock concept + manufacturer

Finalize crate SKU list with supply chain; get sample from packaging vendor; confirm lead time against go-live. Reconcile against real inventory before promising units.

Wk 2-3

Source + score 500 creators

Pull from CRM, run fit model, over-source 20% to absorb no-replies. Legal pre-clears any FTC/gifting-disclosure language.

Wk 3-4

Outreach + address capture

Personalized DMs, secure shipping consent + W-9 where needed, dedupe addresses, flag minors out (18+ only, no exceptions).

Wk 5

Assembly + QC + wave ship

Staggered ship in 4 waves so content doesn't all land same day. Track numbers by tier; hold buffer stock for reships.

Wk 6-8

Capture + measure

Monitor organic posts, log EMV + engagement per creator, ID the top 10% for paid follow-up partnerships.

02
Brand safety reads Clear, or Monitor with a written mitigation attached.
03
Audience demo matches the brand target, checked in-platform, not assumed.
04
The product fits their format without forcing a stiff ad read.
